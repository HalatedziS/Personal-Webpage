Historic Places in Johannesburg

Mandela House

The Mandela House at 71 Vilakazi Street, Soweto, was once the residence of Nelson Mandela and is now a museum dedicated to his legacy. It provides insights into the Mandela family's life during the apartheid era and the struggles they faced. The house is filled with memorabilia, paintings, and photographs that tell the story of the Mandela family.

Constitution Hill

Constitution Hill is a former prison and military fort that bears witness to South Africa's turbulent past and its transition to democracy. It is now home to the Constitutional Court and a major human rights precinct. The site includes the Old Fort, Women's Jail, and Number Four Museum, each with its own significant history.
